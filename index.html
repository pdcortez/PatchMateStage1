Patch Plain

Introducing PatchMate's 'Patch Plain' - a sleek and minimalist design that caters to individuals who appreciate both style and security. Crafted for those who value understated elegance, this discreet patch seamlessly conceals the safety chip within the fabric of your shirt, ensuring it remains virtually unnoticeable to others. With 'Patch Plain,' you can go about your day with confidence, knowing that your safety is in capable hands while maintaining a refined, unobtrusive appearance.

Patch Chip

Introducing PatchMate's 'Patch Chip' - a compact and discrete solution designed exclusively to offer our clients the core technology behind our safety innovations. The Patch Chip is the ideal choice for those who appreciate a minimalist design, as it currently encapsulates the essential safety features while leaving room for engineers to continually refine and enhance its capabilities.

Patch Design

Introducing PatchMate's 'Patch Design' - a trendy and fashionable accessory that not only adds a cool, stylish element to your attire but also seamlessly integrates our innovative safety chip. The Patch Design is the perfect choice for clients who desire a chic and on-trend patch while retaining the essential safety features, offering both fashion and functionality in one captivating package.
